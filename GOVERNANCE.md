# GOVERNANCE

## Lazy Consensus Timeboxes
- **Routine**: 72 hours
- **Governance**: 7 days

## OSNA-style Non-Assert Pledge Requirements
- Participants agree to share information openly and constructively, fostering an inclusive environment.
- Each member pledges to respect the decisions made by the group, ensuring actions are collectively supported.
- Contributions should be made in a way that promotes transparency and accountability.
- Any member may make a request to challenge a decision made under the lazy consensus framework, which must then be addressed in the next governance meeting.

## Community Forum & Founders Group

Governance discussions may take place in **GitHub Discussions** before being formalised as Issues or Pull Requests. See [`FORUM.md`](FORUM.md) for:
- Discussion categories and their intended use
- Founding Cohort membership criteria and how to join
- Patent-free science advocacy threads
- Global reach / internationalisation coordination

The same lazy-consensus timeboxes apply to Discussion-based proposals.
